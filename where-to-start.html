Where to Start

If you’re new to these albums, here are some great entry points across different styles:

  • Fleet Foxes – Self Titled: Warm, harmony-rich folk that’s instantly approachable.

  • Aphex Twin – Selected Ambient Works 85–92: A landmark electronic record, both dreamy and playful.

  • Bob Dylan – Blonde on Blonde: Storytelling and surrealism from the folk-rock master.

  • Beach House – Bloom: Dream pop perfection with soaring melodies.

  • Kendrick Lamar – Good Kid, m.A.A.d City: A modern classic in hip-hop storytelling.
